# Chapter 16.3 prerequisites review

## Missing prerequisites

- **Lesson 16.3.1** — Architectural Principle #6 numbering collision. The lesson declares "Architectural Principle #6 lands here" for the "per-feature, never global ambient" store rule: "The 'per-feature, never global ambient' principle. Architectural Principle #6 lands here." However, Lesson 5.2.3 already introduces and names Architectural Principle #6 as "prefer explicit over magic." The same number is bound to two different principles; by the time a student reaches 16.3, they know #6 as a directive-legibility principle, not a store-scoping principle. Suggested source: assign a new, unused principle number to the per-feature store rule (or check whether the full principle sequence has a gap). Severity: high.
