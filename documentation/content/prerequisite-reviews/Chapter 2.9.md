Server Actions are not introduced until Unit 7 (Chapter 7.2). A student at the Unit 2 boundary has no referent for the concept. Suggested source: a one-line forward-mention of Server Actions in the chapter framing, or replace the example with a generic "API endpoint." Severity: low.

Route handlers are introduced in Chapter 3.6 (Unit 3), which comes after Unit 2. Same sentence as the Server Action reference above. Suggested source: defer or replace with a generic term, or add a parenthetical "covered in Unit 3." Severity: low.
